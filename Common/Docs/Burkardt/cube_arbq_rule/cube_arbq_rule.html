CUBE_ARBQ_RULE
Quadrature Rules for Cubes.

CUBE_ARBQ_RULE is which returns quadrature rules, with exactness up to total degree 15, over the interior of a cube in 3D, by Hong Xiao and Zydrunas Gimbutas.

The original source code, from which this library was developed, is available from the Courant Mathematics and Computing Laboratory, at http://www.cims.nyu.edu/cmcl/quadratures/quadratures.html ,

Related Data and Programs:

ANNULUS_RULE, which computes a quadrature rule for estimating integrals of a function over the interior of a circular annulus in 2D.

CUBE_EXACTNESS, which investigates the polynomial exactness of quadrature rules over the interior of a cube in 3D.

CUBE_GRID, which computes a grid of points over the interior of a cube in 3D.

CUBE_INTEGRALS, which returns the exact value of the integral of any monomial over the interior of the unit cube in 3D.

CUBE_MONTE_CARLO, which applies a Monte Carlo method to estimate the integral of a function over the interior of the unit cube in 3D;

GNUPLOT, C++ programs which illustrate how a program can write data and command files so that gnuplot can create plots of the program results.

PYRAMID_FELIPPA_RULE, which returns Felippa's quadratures rules for approximating integrals over the interior of a pyramid in 3D.

SQUARE_ARBQ_RULE, which returns quadrature rules, with exactness up to total degree 20, over the interior of the symmetric square in 2D, by Hong Xiao and Zydrunas Gimbutas.

SQUARE_FELIPPA_RULE, which returns the points and weights of a Felippa quadrature rule over the interior of a square in 2D.

SQUARE_SYMQ_RULE, which returns symmetric quadrature rules, with exactness up to total degree 20, over the interior of the symmetric square in 2D, by Hong Xiao and Zydrunas Gimbutas.

STROUD, which defines quadrature rules for a variety of M-dimensional regions, including the interior of the square, cube and hypercube, the pyramid, cone and ellipse, the hexagon, the M-dimensional octahedron, the circle, sphere and hypersphere, the triangle, tetrahedron and simplex, and the surface of the circle, sphere and hypersphere.

TETRAHEDRON_FELIPPA_RULE, which returns Felippa's quadratures rules for approximating integrals over the interior of a tetrahedron in 3D.

TRIANGLE_FEKETE_RULE, which defines Fekete rules for interpolation or quadrature over the interior of a triangle in 2D.

TRIANGLE_FELIPPA_RULE, which returns Felippa's quadratures rules for approximating integrals over the interior of a triangle in 2D.

TRIANGLE_SYMQ_RULE, which returns efficient symmetric quadrature rules, with exactness up to total degree 50, over the interior of an arbitrary triangle in 2D, by Hong Xiao and Zydrunas Gimbutas.

WEDGE_FELIPPA_RULE, which returns quadratures rules for approximating integrals over the interior of the unit wedge in 3D.
