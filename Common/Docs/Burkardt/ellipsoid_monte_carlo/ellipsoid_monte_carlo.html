ELLIPSOID_MONTE_CARLO
Monte Carlo Estimate of Integrals in an Ellipsoid

ELLIPSOID_MONTE_CARLO is which uses the Monte Carlo method to estimate the value of an integral over the interior of an ellipsoid in M dimensions.
