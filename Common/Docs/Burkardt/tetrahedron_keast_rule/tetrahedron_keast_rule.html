TETRAHEDRON_KEAST_RULE
Quadrature Rules for a Tetrahedron

TETRAHEDRON_KEAST_RULE is which defines ten quadrature rules, of degree of exactness 0 through 8, over the interior of the tetrahedron in 3D.

The ten rules have the following orders and precisions:
RuleOrderPrecision
1 1 1
2 4 2
3 5 3
410 3
511 4
614 4
715 5
824 6
931 7
1045 8
